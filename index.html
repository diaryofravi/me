I grew up in Goa, surrounded by nature, quiet lanes, and the sea. Those early years taught me to enjoy life’s simple moments—watching sunsets on the beach, getting wet in the rain, and finding peace in the small things around me.

Nature and spirituality guide me deeply. I’m a devotee of Shiva, but I don’t believe in show-off—only in living with honesty, respect, and intention.

This space is where I share what matters to me: balance, simplicity, and the journey of becoming a better version of myself.
